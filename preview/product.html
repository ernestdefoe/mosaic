Avocado is a modern, organic Flarum 2 theme built for forums that care about feel as much as function. It pairs DM Sans typography with generous border-radii and a calm, layered color palette that works equally well in light and dark mode.

What's included

  • Full theme — header, nav, discussion list, post cards, composer, profile pages
  • Custom widgets — trending, top contributors, online users, status pills
  • Dark mode — automatic via prefers-color-scheme, with hand-tuned dark tokens
  • Responsive — mobile, tablet, and desktop layouts tested across 8 viewport sizes
  • Customizer-ready — works seamlessly with the Colored extension
  • Free updates — minor versions free forever, major versions at 50% off

Compatibility

Requires Flarum 2.0 or later and PHP 8.3+. Tested with all official Flarum extensions and the 50 most popular community extensions.
